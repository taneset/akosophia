Meet Our Team

At AkoSophia, every team member combines extensive academic and industry experience. We are proud to offer a rare blend of PhD-level expertise and real-world AI application—a combination that sets us apart in the AI space.

Founder & CEO

Neşet holds a PhD in Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on generative AI, from the University of Auckland. He has worked as a researcher, lecturer, director, and lead developer in both academia and industry, contributing to innovative, industry-focused projects. He has collaborated with prestigious AI institutions, including the Allen Institute for AI and Microsoft AI. Neşet holds a second PhD degree in Mathematics.
